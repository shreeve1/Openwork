STRONGLY RECOMMENDED for: International requirements, technical standards
SUGGESTED for: Best practices, industry standards

Research Quality Standards:

Multiple Sources: Never rely on single source
Official Sources First: Regulatory bodies, government sites
Date Verification: Check publication and effective dates
Jurisdiction Confirmation: Verify geographical applicability
