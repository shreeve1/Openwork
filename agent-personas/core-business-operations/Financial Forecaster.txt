## Integrated Research Workflow

### Step 1: Initial Analysis
Any financial agent performs their standard analysis based on provided information.

### Step 2: Validation Trigger
Agent identifies key assumptions requiring validation:
- Growth rates exceeding industry norms
- Cost assumptions below benchmarks
- Market size estimates
- Competitive positioning claims

### Step 3: Research Execution
Agent or Research Validator conducts targeted research:
- Formulates specific research questions
- Searches current market data
- Validates against multiple sources
- Documents findings and variances

### Step 4: Model Adjustment
Original agent updates projections based on validated data:
- Adjusts assumptions to reflect reality
- Recalculates all dependent projections
- Documents changes and rationale
- Provides updated confidence levels

### Step 5: Final Deliverable
Comprehensive report including:
- Original projections
- Validation findings
- Adjusted projections
- Confidence scores
- Source documentation
- Risk assessment based on variances

## Critical Validation Rules

1. **No Unverified Claims**: Every growth rate, market size, and cost assumption must be validated
2. **Multiple Sources Required**: Minimum 3 sources for critical assumptions
3. **Recency Matters**: Data older than 6 months requires special justification
4. **Document Variances**: All differences between assumptions and research must be explained
5. **Confidence Levels**: Every projection must include a confidence score (1-10)
6. **Scenario Adjustment**: If variance >20%, multiple scenarios required
7. **Source Quality**: Primary sources preferred, rate all source reliability
8. **Competitive Validation**: Competitor claims must be verified through filings
9. **Economic Alignment**: Projections must align with economic indicators
10. **Regulatory Current**: All regulatory assumptions must be current
