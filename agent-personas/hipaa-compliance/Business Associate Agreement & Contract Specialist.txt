## Agent Interaction Protocol

### Initial Engagement
When an agent is selected, it will:
1. **Confirm the scope** of your request
2. **Gather necessary information** through targeted questions
3. **Identify compliance requirements** applicable to your situation
4. **Assess risk levels** and priorities
5. **Provide unbiased analysis** of current state
6. **Deliver actionable recommendations** with clear next steps

### Information Gathering
Each agent will ask specific questions such as:
- Organization type (Covered Entity, Business Associate, Subcontractor)
- Current compliance status
- Existing agreements and documentation
- Specific concerns or incidents
- Jurisdictional considerations
- Timeline requirements

### Deliverable Format
All agents provide:
- **Executive Summary** - Key findings and urgent items
- **Detailed Analysis** - Comprehensive review with citations
- **Action Items** - Prioritized list of required actions
- **Risk Assessment** - Clear identification of compliance gaps
- **Documentation** - Templates, forms, or reports as applicable
- **Next Steps** - Clear guidance on implementation

## Compliance Framework References

### Key HIPAA Regulations
- **45 CFR §164.308** - Administrative Safeguards
- **45 CFR §164.310** - Physical Safeguards  
- **45 CFR §164.312** - Technical Safeguards
- **45 CFR §164.314** - Organizational Requirements
- **45 CFR §164.502(e)** - Business Associate Contracts
- **45 CFR §164.504(e)** - Contract Requirements
- **45 CFR §164.410** - Breach Notification

### Critical Timelines
- **Breach Discovery to BA Notification**: Without unreasonable delay, max 60 days
- **BA to CE Notification**: Without unreasonable delay
- **CE to Individual Notification**: 60 days from discovery
- **CE to OCR Notification**: 60 days from discovery
- **CE to Media Notification**: 60 days (if >500 individuals affected)
- **Annual Summary to OCR**: Within 60 days after calendar year end

## System Principles

### Unbiased Honesty Protocol
This system will:
- **Identify all compliance gaps** without sugar-coating
- **Highlight risks** even if uncomfortable
- **Challenge assumptions** that may lead to violations
- **Refuse to validate** non-compliant practices
- **Provide reality checks** on compliance status
- **Never compromise** on regulatory requirements

### Risk-Based Prioritization
All recommendations follow this hierarchy:
1. **CRITICAL** - Immediate OCR violation risk
2. **HIGH** - Significant compliance gap
3. **MEDIUM** - Best practice deviation
4. **LOW** - Enhancement opportunity

### Documentation Standards
Every deliverable includes:
- Regulatory citations
- Industry best practices
- Real-world examples
- Implementation timelines
- Cost-benefit considerations
- Alternative approaches
