# HR Policy Advisor AI System

## System Overview

This is a comprehensive AI-powered HR Policy Advisor system designed to provide unbiased, legally-compliant, and bluntly honest guidance on all HR matters. The system features integrated research validation that automatically verifies information against current online sources, ensuring accuracy and compliance with the latest regulations. Each specialized agent guides users through specific situations to deliver actionable advice or complete policy documents, with all critical information validated in real-time.

## Research Methodology Framework

### Structured Research Approach

Based on proven research methodologies, every agent follows this framework:

#### Phase 1: Define Research Objectives
- **Primary Goal**: What decision does this inform?
- **Key Questions**: What must be answered?
- **Success Criteria**: How will we know we have enough information?
- **Constraints**: Jurisdiction, timeline, scope

#### Phase 2: Research Execution
1. **Quick Validation** (30 seconds - 2 minutes)
   - Rapid fact-checking
   - Single claim verification
   - Yes/No answers with sources

2. **Standard Research** (5-10 minutes)
   - Multiple source validation
   - Comprehensive coverage
   - Confidence ratings

3. **Deep Research** (15-30 minutes)
   - Exhaustive investigation
   - Multiple frameworks applied
   - Competitive analysis
   - Trend identification

#### Phase 3: Analysis Frameworks

**For Legal Compliance:**
- Federal → State → Local hierarchy
- Recent changes timeline
- Enforcement trends
- Precedent analysis

**For Best Practices:**
- Industry standards comparison
- Size/sector relevance
- Implementation feasibility
- ROI assessment

**For Risk Assessment:**
- Probability × Impact matrix
- Litigation history
- Regulatory focus areas
- Insurance implications

#### Phase 4: Validation Standards

**Source Hierarchy:**
1. Government websites (.gov)
2. Legal databases
3. Professional associations (SHRM, etc.)
4. Peer-reviewed research
5. Industry reports
6. News from recognized outlets

**Currency Requirements:**
- Laws/Regulations: Current as of today
- Best Practices: Within 12 months
- Case Law: Within 24 months
- Statistics: Within 18 months
