  - Competitor information
  - Industry trends or statistics
  - Pricing benchmarks
  - Technology recommendations

### Research Validation Process

```yaml
validation_workflow:
  1_initial_claim:
    action: Identify claim or recommendation needing validation
    
  2_research_execution:
    method: web_search
    queries:

      - Source credibility
      - Publication date

    output:

      - Supporting sources
      - Contradictions noted
    
  5_recommendation_update:
    action: Adjust strategy based on validated data
```

## Integrated Research Tasks

### Market Research with Validation

```markdown
# Market Research with Real-Time Validation

## Purpose
Conduct comprehensive market analysis with all data validated against current online sources

## Process

1. **Initial Research Phase**
   - Define research objectives
   - Identify key metrics to validate
   - Set confidence thresholds

2. **Web Search Validation**
   For each market claim:
   - Execute targeted web searches
   - Gather multiple sources (minimum 3)
   - Check publication dates (prefer < 6 months old)
   - Note contradictory information
   
3. **Market Sizing Validation**
   - TAM: Verify with industry reports via web search
   - SAM: Cross-reference with competitor data
   - SOM: Validate with realistic capture rates
   
4. **Competitive Intelligence**
   - Search for latest competitor updates
   - Verify pricing through online research
   - Check recent product launches
   - Validate market share claims
   
5. **Trend Verification**
   - Search for trend confirmation from multiple sources
   - Check trend trajectory (growing/declining)
   - Verify with recent data points
   
6. **Confidence Scoring**
   Each finding receives confidence score:
   - HIGH (90%+): Multiple recent sources agree
   - MEDIUM (70-89%): Some sources agree, minor variations
   - LOW (<70%): Limited or conflicting sources
   - UNVERIFIED: Cannot validate with current search

## Deliverables
- Validated Market Research Report
- Source Documentation
- Confidence Scores for All Claims
- Contradictions and Uncertainties Noted
```
