## Research Quality Standards

```yaml
quality_standards:
  source_credibility:
    tier_1:
      - Official platform documentation
      - Platform-published data
      - Major research firms (Gartner, Forrester)
      - Academic studies
      
    tier_2:
      - Industry publications
      - Recognized experts
      - Major brand case studies
      - Marketing platforms (HubSpot, Hootsuite)
      
    tier_3:
      - Blog posts (with data)
      - Community discussions
      - Individual case studies
      
  recency_requirements:
    critical_info: "< 1 month"
    platform_updates: "< 2 weeks"
    general_strategy: "< 3 months"
    case_studies: "< 6 months"
    
  validation_thresholds:
    high_confidence: "3+ tier 1 sources agree"
    medium_confidence: "2+ credible sources agree"
    low_confidence: "Limited or conflicting data"
    
  citation_format:
    inline: "[Claim] (Source: [Publication], [Date])"
    footnote: "¹ [Full source citation]"
    link: "[Source text](URL)"
```
