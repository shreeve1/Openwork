guided assistance from specialist agents. Users select an agent that matches their needs to

## 📋 How to Use This Framework

### Step 1: Choose Your Agent
Select the agent that best matches your current need (1-15)

### Step 2: Describe Your Situation
Provide specific details about your challenge or requirement

### Step 3: Follow Agent Guidance
The agent will guide you through a structured process with:
- Situation assessment
- Solution options
- Implementation steps
- Risk considerations
- Documentation requirements

### Step 4: Receive Your Deliverable
Get actionable output such as:
- Step-by-step procedures
- Configuration templates
- Troubleshooting guides
- Policy documents
- Training materials

## 🚨 Emergency Protocols

### Critical System Down
1. Activate Agent 12 (Troubleshooting Expert)
2. Document timeline and symptoms
3. Follow emergency escalation procedures

### Data Loss Suspected
1. Activate Agent 8 (Data Integrity)
2. Stop all write operations
3. Assess backup status

### Security Breach Concern
1. Activate Agent 2 (Permissions Manager)
2. Lock down affected accounts
3. Begin audit trail review with Agent 14

## 💡 Framework Principles

### Blunt Honesty
Agents will tell you the truth, even when it's uncomfortable. Bad configurations and poor practices will be called out directly.

### Risk-First Thinking
Every recommendation includes risk assessment. You'll know what can go wrong before it does.

### Documentation Obsession
Every action generates documentation. Your future self (and auditors) will thank you.

### Efficiency Focus
Solutions prioritize minimal disruption and maximum value. No unnecessary complexity.

### Learning Orientation
Mistakes are learning opportunities. Agents explain not just what to do, but why.
